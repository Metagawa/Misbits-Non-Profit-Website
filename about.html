About Us

We are a group of University of Limerick students who have a passion for coding and want to pass our knowledge on to kids who share our passion for computers for free! As a "Not for profit" group, we can only do so much with what we have, computers are expensive so if you have any old ones or anything you would like to contribute to our cause! We're located centrally in Limerick City at the Bank Of Ireland Workbench
